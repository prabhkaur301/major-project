Fake Content Detector

Verify variety of content being shared on internet - textual posts, news , url using our website.

Features of this Project

Fake News Detector

The fake news on internet is wide spreading & is a matter of serious concern due to its ability to cause a lot of social and national damage with destructive impacts. This application makes use of Machine Learning and NLP to detect whether news is Fake or Real

Malicious URL Detector

Malicious URLs host unsolicited content (spam, phishing, drive-by downloads, etc.) and lure unsuspecting users to become victims of scams. This feature is based of Machine Learning Classifier and predict whether the URL is malicious or safe.

Twitter Sentiment Analysis

Twitter sentiment analysis identifies negative, positive, or neutral emotions within the text of a tweet using natural language processing (NLP) and machine learning. Helpful for companies to get better understanding of the social sentiment of its brand, product, or service.

Objectives of This Project

  • To verify variety of content being shared on internet - textual posts, news, URLs using Machine learning and NLP techniques
  • To provide sentiment analysis of the textual contents
  • To detect malicious url being shared on internet and prevent user to visit phishing sites
